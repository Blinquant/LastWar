Troop Composition Strategy

  1. Scout Enemy First: Always check what troops your opponent has before attacking.
  2. Counter Their Composition: Most of players use Tanks, so play Aircraft :)
  3. As Free-To-Play Player: Focus on one Type of squad.
  4. Overloard: Use your monkey with your T1.

Pro-Level Strategies

  1. Use gathering to travel: Gathering near the spot your destination is faster than moving there by attacking
  2. Rally Coordination: Work with alliance members to launch coordinated attacks on strong targets.
  3. Terrain Advantage: Use the world map to your advantage - spawn near areas where others can't.
  4. Unevitable defeat: Don't defend if you know you will lose anyway.
