Gear Crafting Guide

Essential Rules

Focus on UR Gear

Only invest resources in UR (Ultra Rare) gear for maximum efficiency and power.

Level in Increments

Gear stats are boosted only by upgrades occurring every 10 levels.

Buy Blueprints

Always purchase blueprints whenever available to ensure steady progression.
