Advanced Strategy Event Guides

Comprehensive playbooks for each stage of your Last War journey

Strategy Guide: Winter Storm Battlefield

Transition from standard warfare to competitive battlefield operations. Success relies on controlling objectives, maximizing points and maintaining troop efficiency.

Step-by-Step Strategy

  1. Control Key Structures: Upon entering the battlefield, immediately prioritize Missile Sites and Oil Wells. These locations generate continuous Battlefield Points. The first team to reach 30,000 points wins.
  2. Focus on Timing: If the timer expires, victory goes to the side with the highest score. Coordinate captures carefully to hold more structures as the battle closes.
  3. Central Silo Domination: The Silo opens after a short period. Capturing and holding it grants massive point boosts.
  4. Secure Supply Airdrops: Resource crates will drop periodically across the map. Send fast-moving units to collect them, these provide both points and tactical advantages.
  5. Unit Deployment System: On entering, your highest-level units automatically fill the Independent Drill Ground. These units are unique to the battlefield and won't affect your base's army composition.
  6. No Permanent Losses: In this event, units do not die. Severely wounded troops are automatically healed for free after the battle. Take advantage of this to play aggressively and dominate objectives.

Strategy Guide: Desert Storm

Transition from standard alliance warfare to large-scale battlefield operations. Victory depends on coordination, timing and control of strategic objectives.

Step-by-Step Strategy

  1. Prepare Your Team: At the beginning of each battle, a role with a number is assigned to you, it's important to remember it. Balance roles between Vanguard, Logistics Support, Healer,...
  2. Stage 1 - Establish Control: Capture Oil Refineries, Science Hubs, and Field Hospitals first. These provide early Battlefield Points and strengthen your alliance's starting advantage.
  3. Stage 2 - Push for Central Objectives: Once secured, move toward the Nuclear Silo, Arsenal, and Mercenary Factory. These offer the highest point output, control them as soon as they unlock.
  4. Stage 3 - Secure Oil Wells: As Oil Wells appear, if all your units are dead or the game is won, send out your squads to continuously harvest them.
  5. Use Free Teleports Strategically: Relocate every few minutes to capture undefended buildings or reinforce key objectives. Avoid wasting teleports during cooldowns.
  6. Play Aggressively - No Unit Deaths: Since units cannot die, take calculated risks. Push forward during critical moments, knowing your wounded troops will fully recover after battle.
  7. Hold Buildings for Maximum Value: Buildings generate extra Battlefield Points over time. Predict enemy movements by checking our positions with many points.
  8. Earn Individual Points Efficiently: Attack higher tier enemies for more personal points. Garrison buildings (+30 pts/sec), gather at Oil Wells (+5 pts/sec), and collect Supply Boxes to boost your ranking.
  9. Adapt and Communicate: Maintain constant communication through alliance chat. Quick coordination between defense and offense is the key to victory.

Late Game Strategy (Level 26+)

Compete with Server 743's elite players. Focus on optimization, specialization, and strategic dominance.

Step-by-Step Progression

  1. Specialize Your Approach: Choose between offensive raiding, defensive fortress, or balanced gameplay.
  2. Maximize Key Buildings: Prioritize Command Center, army camps, and research facilities to maximum level.
  3. Deploy T4 Troops: The strongest available units. Expensive but essential for top-tier competition.
  4. Compete in Global Rankings: Aim for top positions in power, kills, or resources for recognition and rewards.
  5. Control Territory: Capture and maintain resource tiles on the world map for passive income generation.
  6. Engage in Alliance Wars: Coordinate massive battles against rival factions on Server 743.
  7. Optimize Equipment Sets: Craft and upgrade premium gear for commanders to maximize effectiveness.

PvP Focused Strategy

Dominate Server 743 through aggressive military tactics. This approach suits players who prioritize combat and conquest.

Step-by-Step Progression

  1. Build a War-Centric Base: Prioritize military buildings, hospitals, and troop production over resource generation.
  2. Research Military Technology: Maximize attack, defense, and health bonuses for all troop categories.
  3. Scout All Targets: Always scout before attacking. Understand enemy strength and identify weaknesses.
  4. Execute Hit-and-Run: Attack, loot efficiently, then use shields or relocate to avoid retaliation.
  5. Coordinate Rally Attacks: Work with alliance members for combined assaults on fortified targets.
  6. Employ Counter-Intelligence: Keep army composition secret. Vary strategies against different opponents.
  7. Maintain Hospital Capacity: Upgrade hospitals to minimize casualties and enable faster recovery.
