Requirements for HQ Levels 31 to 35

With the Season 2 Celebration update and its new research tree The Age of Oil, it is now possible to upgrade the HQ up to level 35.

Unlocking HQ 31-35 Upgrade

To access this feature, you must first complete the "HQ Expansion Lv1" research in The Age of Oil tree.

Observed Progression

HQ progression beyond level 30 mainly depends on oil production, limited per hour. Here is the observed progression without purchases:

1 week and 4 hours

Start of Oil Output I research - Level 10

2 weeks and 4 hours

Start of Oil Output II - Level 4, with 50,000 oil units remaining

3 weeks and 4 hours

Start of Oil Output II - Level 8

4 weeks and 4 hours

Start of Oil Output III - Level 1

5 weeks and 4 hours

Start of Oil Output III - Level 10, with 130,000 oil units remaining

6 weeks+

Start of HQ 31 upgrade, without oil purchases
