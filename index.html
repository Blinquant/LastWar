Advanced Strategy Guides

Comprehensive playbooks for each stage of your Last War journey

Early Game Strategy (Level 1-15)

Build a solid foundation during your first days on Server 743. This phase determines your long-term success trajectory.

Step-by-Step Progression

  1. Rush Command Center to Level 10: This unlocks critical buildings and features. Use all available speed-ups to achieve this milestone quickly.
  2. Complete Tutorial Missions: These provide excellent rewards and teach core mechanics. Don't skip any tutorial objectives.
  3. Join an Alliance Immediately: Gain building assistance, protection, and valuable resources. Seek active alliances on Server 743.
  4. Focus on Resource Production: Upgrade farms, oil refineries, and steel mills evenly. Target at least level 8 for each facility.
  5. Train Troops Continuously: Never leave barracks idle. Build a balanced army of infantry and vehicles.
  6. Claim All Free Rewards: Check mail, events, and daily missions regularly for speed-ups and resources.
  7. Utilize Shield Protection: Don't waste beginner shields. Use them while building defensive capabilities.

Mid Game Strategy (Level 16-25)

Transition from pure expansion to strategic warfare. Balance continued growth with military development and server participation.

Step-by-Step Progression

  1. Diversify Your Army: Unlock and train all troop types. Maintain a versatile composition for different scenarios.
  2. Begin Research Progression: Focus on military and economic branches. Prioritize troop attack and defense bonuses.
  3. Participate in Server Events: Events offer massive rewards. Coordinate with your alliance for maximum points.
  4. Upgrade Hero Units: Heroes are crucial for combat success. Level them up and unlock special abilities.
  5. Produce T3 Troops: These units significantly outperform T2. Begin replacing your army with higher tiers.
  6. Optimize Base Layout: Redesign for defensive strength. Protect Command Center and resource storage.
  7. Farm Inactive Bases: Scout the map for abandoned locations and farm them regularly for resources.

Late Game Strategy (Level 26+)

Compete with Server 743's elite players. Focus on optimization, specialization, and strategic dominance.

Step-by-Step Progression

  1. Specialize Your Approach: Choose between offensive raiding, defensive fortress, or balanced gameplay.
  2. Maximize Key Buildings: Prioritize Command Center, army camps, and research facilities to maximum level.
  3. Deploy T4 Troops: The strongest available units. Expensive but essential for top-tier competition.
  4. Compete in Global Rankings: Aim for top positions in power, kills, or resources for recognition and rewards.
  5. Control Territory: Capture and maintain resource tiles on the world map for passive income generation.
  6. Engage in Alliance Wars: Coordinate massive battles against rival factions on Server 743.
  7. Optimize Equipment Sets: Craft and upgrade premium gear for commanders to maximize effectiveness.

PvP Focused Strategy

Dominate Server 743 through aggressive military tactics. This approach suits players who prioritize combat and conquest.

Step-by-Step Progression

  1. Build a War-Centric Base: Prioritize military buildings, hospitals, and troop production over resource generation.
  2. Research Military Technology: Maximize attack, defense, and health bonuses for all troop categories.
  3. Scout All Targets: Always scout before attacking. Understand enemy strength and identify weaknesses.
  4. Execute Hit-and-Run: Attack, loot efficiently, then use shields or relocate to avoid retaliation.
  5. Coordinate Rally Attacks: Work with alliance members for combined assaults on fortified targets.
  6. Employ Counter-Intelligence: Keep army composition secret. Vary strategies against different opponents.
  7. Maintain Hospital Capacity: Upgrade hospitals to minimize casualties and enable faster recovery.
